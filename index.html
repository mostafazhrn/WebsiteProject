About Us

Best lawyers in Egypt | lawyers Cairo

The office was originally established in 1998 under the name of "The International Office For Legal Consultations", and later on, Mr. Ahmed Zahran came on board acting as managing partner of the office which name has changed to "Zahran Law Office".

Zahran Law Office is a multi-disciplinary business law firm open to the world. A team of specialist lawyers providing counsel as well as contentious and transactional services while sharing its clients' spirit of entrepreneurship.

We devote all our efforts, time, and experience to the service of the clients and we determine the main lines of work according to the client requirements and the provisions of law. The client is at the center of the firm’s attention. We establish cooperative relationships with our clients, based on responsiveness and close contact.

We believe that an appropriate legal response is not only based on law, but, above all, on extending and developing our clients’ business. For this end, we use law as an instrument to provide our clients with inventive and customized solutions.
